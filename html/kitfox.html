KitFox

Overview

KitFox is a 48 key custom keyboard in an ortholinear alice layout featuring two 6.25u spacebars and fr4 plate and back panel. Compatible with any pro micro footprint microcontroller in addition to wireless controllers like the nice!nano. To accommodate this, KitFox incorporates a power circuit into the pcb to host a battery.

Features

[48 hotswap MX keys]
[smd diodes]
[wireless support]
[reset and power switches]
